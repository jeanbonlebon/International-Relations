The IUT (Universitary and technological Institute) of Troyes was created in 1968, it functions within the University of Reims Champagne-Ardenne.

It was initially destined to train students for a DUT (Universitary and Technological Degree) but it extended itself to other formations and and more recently to professional degrees.

The IUT of Troyes proposes a DUT in many different postgraduate courses.

The DUT is considered an initial formation as it is directly accessible after the obtention of your A-Levels (General, Professional..)

In a DUT, you can chose between 6 different formations, that will take 2 years for you to complete.

What is a DUT ?

A DUT (Universitary and Technological Degree) is :

  • A full time studying cycle
  • Internships in both schoolyears
  • Both practical and theory classes
  • A schedule with classic type holidays
  • The ability to have a second job outside of classes

Whatever DUT cursus you chose, you have the ability to go abroad for a year, to study in one of the partnered universities of the IUT.

In anticipation of a year of studying abroad, it is recommended to take the TOEIC test. It’ll help determine your English speaking skills, to stand out in the market and it’ll always be a good point on your resume.

Companies are always on the lookout for students with a certain English level, as it remains the main international language today.
